VideoOverlay

The VideoOverlay class creates a display area for playing MPEG-1, WMV, H.264, Theora, etc.
It can also be displayed on a KiriKiri layer.
krdstheora.dll is required to play Theora.
Except for layer playback, in overlay mode, the VideoOverlay display area is shown in front of all layers and cannot be transparent.
Layer playback typically puts a higher load on the processor than overlay playback.
On Android, it is limited to a mode equivalent to an overlay that is always displayed in the foreground.
Also, the playable formats depend on the device model.
Many devices seem to be able to play H.264.
On Android, video files are stored in assets.
